The Mayenne Canal

Jul 19th, 2014

If I were to live this life again it would be as a lock keeper on the river Mayenne that rises in Normandy and flows south almost unnoticed to join with the Sarthe and the Loire.

Out of reach of the wake of life I would enjoy what strikes me as the ideal existence in my charming two-bedroomed house on a peaceful riverbank. With no rent to pay, a modest salary of 600 euros per month and no boss in the immediate vicinity it all seems to me to add up to a whole lot of priceless contentment.
